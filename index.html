Action Plan: Phishing Link

  • Do NOT click: Close the website immediately.
  • Check URL: Look for typos (e.g., 'amaz0n.com').
  • Credentials: If you entered a password, change it now.
  • Report: Flag the message as spam.

Action Plan: Smishing (SMS Scam)

  • Do NOT reply: Confirming your number leads to more spam.
  • Verify independently: Call the bank using the number on your card.
  • Ignore threats: "Account Suspended" is a common scare tactic.
  • No installs: Never download apps from SMS links.

    Learn About Scams

    Understanding scams helps you avoid them. Below are the most common techniques used by attackers:

    • Urgency Pressure: Messages that say “your account will close in 30 minutes.”
    • Fake Rewards: “You won a prize!” scams to trick you.
    • Credential Theft: Fake login pages stealing passwords.
    • Identity Spoofing: Attackers pretending to be banks or government.
